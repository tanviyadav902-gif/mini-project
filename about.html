About us:

Bombay Velvet is a vibrant lounge-bar café suited for casual evenings,
live music, and group hangouts. While it's popular for its drinks and
atmosphere, the food and service quality reportedly vary. If you're in the
mood for a relaxed, energetic venue, it's worth checking out—but don’t
expect haute cuisine.

                                  

  • The menu spans Italian, Chinese, Continental, Biryani, North & South Indian preparations.
  • Renovated with a vibrant, nostalgic décor — ideal for groups and nights out. Known for its music, seating space, and youthful vibe.
  • Offers include flat 10% off, scratch cards, and bank card discounts on reservations or dining.
